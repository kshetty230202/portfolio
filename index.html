My process is structured for delivering robust, production-ready solutions. It begins with a deep dive into your goals, followed by data analysis, model prototyping, and rigorous validation. The final stage is deploying the model into a scalable architecture with continuous monitoring to ensure peak performance.

Reliability is non-negotiable. I ensure it through several key practices: creating resilient data pipelines with strict validation, iterative model tuning based on real-world feedback, and implementing comprehensive logging and monitoring to track performance and detect anomalies before they become problems.

I specialize in a modern, production-focused tech stack. For backend services, I use Python, FastAPI, and Docker. For machine learning, my tools of choice are PyTorch, TensorFlow, and Scikit-learn. In Generative AI, I leverage LLMs, RAG architectures, and vector databases like FAISS.

To get started, I typically need three things: a clear definition of the problem you're trying to solve, access to the relevant data (under NDA, of course), and a high-level understanding of what a successful outcome looks like for your business. From there, we can build a detailed project plan.
